# 🌌 Cosmic Voyage

An interactive journey through the universe — from Earth's orbit to the edge of the observable cosmos.

**[✨ Start the journey](https://cosmic-voyage.github.io/)** *(hosted with GitHub Pages)*

- 8 stages of travel: Earth orbit → the night sky's constellations → the planets → the stars → nebulae & black holes → galaxies & the whole universe
- 39 clickable objects, each with real NASA/ESA/ESO imagery, key stats and fun facts
- Ambient music generated live in your browser
- 📏 Scale mode: pinch or scroll to zoom from the ISS to the observable universe, everything at true relative size
- 🚀 3D Flight: fly from the Sun to Pluto in real 3D at [/fly/](https://cosmic-voyage.github.io/fly/)
- 🌙 Tonight's Sky: a live sky chart for your location plus a full-screen "point at the sky" planetarium (drag to look, or point your phone) — Sun, Moon, planets & bright stars at their real positions
- 📅 Cosmic Calendar: the universe's 13.8 billion years compressed into a single scrubbable year
- Track how much of the universe you've explored

Built as a single self-contained HTML file. Imagery courtesy of NASA, ESA/Hubble, and ESO.
